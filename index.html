Federal GST/HST Rebate ?
Calculated as 36% of the federal GST/HST paid, capped at $6,300 (5% GST / 13%/15% HST). Reduced on a sliding scale when FMV is $350K–$450K. Zero at $450K+.
 $0
Ontario Provincial Rebate ?
75% of the provincial part of HST paid (8/13 of total HST), capped at $24,000 if you paid HST on the land, or $16,080 if you did not. Available even when FMV ≥ $450K.
