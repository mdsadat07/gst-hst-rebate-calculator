📊 Complete Benefit Breakdown
RC4028 Federal GST/HST Rebate ?
36% of federal GST/HST paid, capped at $6,300. Phase-out: FMV $350K–$450K sliding scale, $0 at $450K+. Available to all buyers. Current law — CRA Guide RC4028.
$0 ✅ Current Law
Bill C-4 First-Time Buyer GST Rebate ?
Full refund of 5% federal GST on new homes ≤$1M. Sliding phase-out $1M–$1.5M. First-time buyers only. Purchase agreements signed March 20, 2025–Dec 31, 2030. Bill C-4 received Royal Assent March 12, 2026.
— ✅ Royal Assent Mar 12 2026
Provincial HST Rebate ?
Ontario: 75% of 8% provincial HST, capped $24,000 (with land HST) or $16,080. Available in ON, NS, NB, NL, PE only.
$0 ✅ Current Law (ON)
Nova Scotia First-Time Buyers Rebate Claim separately via Service NS
First-Time Home Buyers' Tax Credit (HBTC) ?
Federal income tax credit: $10,000 × 15% = $1,500 tax reduction. Claimed on your T1 income tax return for the year of purchase. First-time buyers only.
$0 ✅ Current Law
Land Transfer Tax Rebate ?
Ontario first-time buyer rebate up to $4,000 on provincial LTT. Toronto also has a Municipal LTT with a separate rebate up to $4,475. Other provinces vary.
$0 ✅ Current Law

Estimated income tax savings from FHSA contributions used toward this purchase. Based on your entered marginal tax rate × total contributions. Actual savings depend on your tax situation.
